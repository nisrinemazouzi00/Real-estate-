FAQ

How can I modify or cancel a property booking?

To modify or cancel a property booking, log into your account, navigate to the "My Bookings" section, select the booking, and choose the appropriate option to modify or cancel. Cancellation fees may apply based on the property's policy.

What is the process for taking possession of a property?

The possession process depends on the property type and contract terms. Typically, possession occurs on the date specified in the rental or purchase agreement. Ensure all necessary payments and documentation are complete.

How can I pay rent for my property?

Rent payments can be made directly through our platform using secure payment options such as credit/debit cards or bank transfers. Access the "My Rentals" section and select "Pay Rent" for the relevant property.

How do I communicate with potential buyers?

When a buyer expresses interest in your property, you will receive a notification via email or your dashboard. Use the platform's secure messaging system to communicate directly with the buyer.

Why isn't my property listing visible on the platform?

If your property listing is not visible, it may be under review, missing required details, or in violation of platform guidelines. Check your dashboard for any alerts or updates regarding the listing status.

What steps can I take to promote my property listing?

To increase the visibility of your property listing, consider using the "Featured Listing" option available in your dashboard. This option highlights your property, making it more visible to potential buyers or renters.
